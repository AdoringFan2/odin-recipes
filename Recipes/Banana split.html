Banana split

A simple yet tasty dessert to make for your kids !

Here is the differents ingredients and steps to make this wonderful dessert.

Ingredients

  • Bananas
  • Whipped cream
  • The syrup of your choice
  • The Ice cream of your choice
  • Almonds

Cooking process steps

  1. Break the almonds into little pieces with a rolling pin
  2. Cut the banana in half and put it in your dish
  3. Make three scoops of ice cream and put it in the middle of the split banana
  4. Put a lot of whipped cream on the banana split
  5. Add the syrup all over the whipped cream and ice cream
  6. Put the almonds you broke before all over your banana split
