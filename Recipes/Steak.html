Steak

A good juicy steak to make for you and you only !

Here is the list of ingredients and steps to make this tasty steak.

Ingredients

  • Steak
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Rosemary
  1. Put butter in a hot pan
  2. Put the steak in the hot pan and add salt
  3. Once it's cooked, put Rosemary on it and serve it
