Lasagna

A nice meaty Lasagna for the whole family, easy to make !

Here is the list to make a whole dish of lasagna for the family !

Ingredients

  • Long pasta
  • Ground beef
  • Homemade Tomato sauce
  • Cheese
  • Salt
  • Water

Cooking process steps

  1. Boil water and put a pinch of salt into it
  2. Once the water is boiling, put the long pasta into it and cook it for a few minutes
  3. In the meantime, cook slightly the ground meat and add the homemade tomato sauce
  4. Once it's done, take off the long pasta from the boiling water and put it in the dish
  5. Then put the ground beef on the long pasta then spread it all over the dish
  6. After that, put another layer of pasta and repeat the step 5 until the dish is full
  7. To finish the dish, put some cheese on top of the dish and put it in the oven for 30 minutes
